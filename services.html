Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely! Security is our top priority. FinDash uses end-to-end encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures and regularly update our systems to protect against the latest threats.

Can I connect my bank accounts?

Yes, FinDash supports secure connections with over 10,000 financial institutions. Our bank synchronization feature uses bank-level security protocols to ensure your credentials are never stored on our servers.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, FinDash is available on iOS and Android devices. The mobile app offers the same powerful features as the web version, allowing you to manage your finances on the go.

How much does FinDash cost?

FinDash offers a free basic plan with essential features. Premium plans start at $9.99/month and include advanced features like investment tracking, financial forecasting, and priority support.
